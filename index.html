Travel
Getting here:

We recommend flying into Seattle-Tacoma International Airport, which is about 30 minutes from the city by car. Taxis, rideshares, and rental cars are all convenient options for getting around. You can also take the Link light rail directly from the airport into the city, which costs $3.00 and takes about 40-50 minutes.

Where to stay:

We do not have a hotel block reserved, but there are plenty of hotel and Airbnb options in the city to choose from. Some neighborhoods you might want to consider include: Capitol Hill, the University District, and Roosevelt/Ravenna (where we live!), all of which are easily accessible from the light rail.

FAQ
What should I wear?
Dress for the party you are hoping to attend! Semi-formal attire that is fun, comfy, and colorful is encouraged. Our ceremony will be held outdoors on grassy, uneven ground (weather permitting), so choose your shoes accordingly. Dress code for all other events is casual.
Can I bring a plus-one?
Yes! There is space on our RSVP form for you to list a plus-one, if you so choose.
Can I bring my kids?
Children are welcome at all of our events! We just ask that anyone under the age of 16 be supervised by an adult at all times (as per our venue's rules).
Can I bring my dog?
Well-behaved, leashed dogs are also welcome at all of our events.
Will I be able to pet the goats?
Yes! Petting the goats is highly encouraged.
