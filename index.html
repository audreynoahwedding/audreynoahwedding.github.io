FAQ
What should I wear?
Dress for the party you are hoping to attend! Semi-formal attire that is fun, comfy, and colorful is encouraged. Our ceremony will be held outdoors on grassy, uneven ground (weather permitting), so choose your shoes accordingly. Dress code for all other events is casual.
Can I bring a plus-one?
Yes! There will be space on our RSVP form for you to list a plus-one, if you so choose.
Can I bring my kids?
Children are welcome at all of our events! We just ask that anyone under the age of 16 be supervised by an adult at all times (as per our venue's rules).
Can I bring my dog?
Well-behaved, leashed dogs are also welcome at all of our events.
Will I be able to pet the goats?
Yes! Petting the goats is highly encouraged.
